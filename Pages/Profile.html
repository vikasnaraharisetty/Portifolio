A small brief about me

I'm a UI/UX designer based in Hyderabad, India.

Hi, I'm Vikas Naraharisetty and I like to refer to myself as a “Vicky”. I love solving complex UX problems, and finding opportunities for good user experience. Having worked with good assortment of mobile, web, B2C and B2B products, I am highly proficient at conducting user research, building wireframes at varying levels of fidelity and interactive prototypes for establishing and justifying the product direction. My approach to good design is finding the right balance of user empathy, usability, aesthetics and a deep understanding of my users.

I focus on user problems and provide genuine solutions. I specialize in web design, brand identity and UI design and of course... front end development.
