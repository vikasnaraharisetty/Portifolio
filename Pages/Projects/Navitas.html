Navitas website design

Navitas is an US based consulting & software development firm that helps consultants, retain and manage talented people. My mission was to design them a brand new website with what was there while figuring out the content.

Designing the visuals

With the branding already figured out in the old one which really need some love, it was time to start working on the website. The aim here was to design a clean and simple website and compile all the different kinds of servies into the design with an profisional feel to it. When designing a website, one of the first things that needs to be figured out is the content. Content is definitely king. Why did they come to your website? How can you solve their problem? Once the purpose of the website is clear, you can find the most effective way to deliver the message. Content was written based on insights from customer interviews, research and a competitor analysis.

It was clear that we basically needed a simple website that outlined the company’s services and gave potential customers an easy way to get in touch. We decided to go with a simple multipe page website broken up into five sections in the menu with each seaction broken down further as seen in the above. Clicking on a link in the top navigation bar would slide the user down to the appropriate section of the page.
