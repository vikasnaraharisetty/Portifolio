Accountri brand website & Wen application design

Accountri is a complete Business Management Software that makes collaboration smooth across HRM, Project management, Job management, Operations, Document management & Accounting departments. I helped them create a brand, website and web application, and also involved in development activities.

Creating the brand

The word “accountri” derived from accounting, and since the company operates mainly in the Business Management sector, I wanted to incorporate the definition (Management can be defined as the process of administering and controlling the affairs of the organization) of management which is an infinite loop of activities into the logo design. I experimented with quite a few ideas during my logo design process, until finally I came up with one that I thought worked. I used simple circular shapes to combine the cc alphabets in the word “accountri “for the resemblance of infinite.

Designing the visuals

With the branding figured out, it was time to start working on the website and web application. The aim here was to design a clean and simple brochure website and compile all the different kinds of forms into a simle intutative design with an industrial feel to it. We decided that only a small sitemap was necessary as the website would be light on content. But coming to the application it is totally opposite it has lot of content that need to be organised so we managed to pull down the requirements to different modules and go ahead with a simple clean design.

I sketched out a few quick concepts for the homepage and then got straight into Photoshop. We decided to centre the homepage on an image collage that slowly fades from one image to the next. Sharp corners and strong flat colours are used throughout to be consistent with the logo design.

Long term gains

I was greatly appreciative of the opportunity to work with my client to help build them a solid brand and web presence. We enjoyed working together during the creative process and we’re all very happy with the result. I hope their investment brings them long-term gains in the years to come.
