Natyabharati website design & ticket booking

Natyabharati is an US based theater group and is the oldest group in North America. It brings the diversity of Indian theater to theater-lovers of the Washington D.C. metropolitan area. My mission was to design them brand new website while using the old one.

Research & discovery

After talking to the client and doing some research into the organisation, it was clear that the organisation needed to have a professional, corporate aesthetic that still appealed to those in the technology sector. So we planned to go with a theme based website which will be more relevent to the organisation that is theater.

Once we had planned out what we were going to build, the development team was able to start rebuilding the back-end for speed and stability. I started on the interface design in parallel with them to save time, and quickly sketched out some ideas on how this pages would function. My focus was on creating maximal power with minimal UI. The page would basically consist of a slider at the top followed by a list of plays that are available for booking.
